Group travel

Our group tours guarantee you to see the most popular attractions at a comparatively low price.

Besides, it has the following highlight points:

  1. The group size is small, limited to a maximum of 16 to 20 to guarantee the comfortableness and quality of the tour.
  2. Share a clean, safe, and air-conditioned vehicle, with enough personal seated space and luggage space.
  3. Share a guide and driver but their special treatment makes you feel like part of a friendly family.
  4. Good chance for you to meet and make friends from all over the world.
